›› About DartsXL
DartsXL produces dartboards for sale and provides the DartsXL Counter for free.
› About DartsXL Counter
DartsXL Counter is a free, browser-based darts scoring application developed to provide the most comprehensive, fluent, insightful, and accurate 501 darts experience available online.
The DartsXL Counter has an advanced system for checkout routes. It instantly provides the player with sets of checkout routes when they enter a score.
The DartsXL Counter application provides an equally comprehensive set of features supporting the classic dartboard, as it does for the DartsXL Dartboards.
› About DartsXL Dartboards
DartsXL produces dartboards based on new designs: XLBoard, Heptaboard Full and Heptaboard Open. The dartboards are manufactured in high-quality sisal fibres. The wire dividers are precision-crafted and assembled by hand, creating a robust, high-quality dartboard. The new dartboard designs provide a unique experience and new opportunities for dart players.

› Our Goals
The primary goal of the DartsXL Counter is to provide a professional-grade tool for dart players of all skill levels, completely free and with no data collection. The built-in Checkout Routes Analyzer has a dynamic UI for showing outshot finishing paths. It can be used for both analysis of real-time match history and analysis of any checkout routes.
The goal of the DartsXL Dartboards is to provide new types of competitive-level dartboards that can also be enjoyed by players of all skill levels.
DartsXL's goal is to offer a new, competitive, fully immersive experience of playing 501 darts. The DartsXL Counter provides complex checkout routes instantly (including quads), so you can play 501 with full fluency on both the classic dartboard and the DartsXL Dartboards.
