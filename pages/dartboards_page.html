› Heptaboard Open
The Heptaboard Open offers:
• 
Quads, in addition to doubles and trebles.
• 
A heptagon shape (7-sided polygon) and utilizes precise manipulation of its natural 21 segments, to create a clever 20-segment dartboard. With an adjustment to the segment "20" being smaller than on a classic dartboard.
• 
The same segments 20,1 and 5, as on the Heptaboard Full.
• 
A new segment composition: 1-8, 13-17 and 20. Providing 14 numbers for the player instead of 20.
• 
Increases variation in scoring and the number of strategic possibilities greatly. Provides progression to checkouts. Adds complexity to checkout routes, the DartsXL Counter provides perfect fluency of play.
