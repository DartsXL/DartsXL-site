›› Privacy Policy
This policy outlines how DartsXL Counter handles data. Your privacy is important to us.
› Data Collection
We collect zero personal data. The DartsXL Counter application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about its users.
The app operates entirely in your browser and makes no network calls for tracking or data collection purposes.
› Amazon Affiliate Links
This application contains Amazon affiliate links. When you click these links and make purchases, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This helps support this free application and DartsXL.
When you click an Amazon link, Amazon may use cookies and tracking when you visit their site through our links, subject to Amazon's own privacy policy.
› Local Storage
The app may use browser local storage to save your game state, historic matches and settings on your device. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings or in the DartsXL Counter's start screen.
› Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services within the DartsXL Counter application.
› Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
