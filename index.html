› Features Overview
DartsXL Counter is a free, no-download, dart counter web app for 501 darts rules with any score and any darts in hand.
Score by placing darts or entering score. Get immediate checkout routes for any score.
The app supports four dartboard layouts, including the classic dartboard and the DartsXL dartboards.
› Privacy & Data
We collect no personal data. The DartsXL Counter makes no networks calls. The app's only links go to Amazon, from the welcome prompt. Works on all devices.
› Physical Products
The DartsXL dartboards XLBoard, Heptaboard Open and Heptaboard Full are available to purchase on Amazon.
